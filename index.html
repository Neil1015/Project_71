What's New!

  • New School Cafeteria

    The food options and responsibilities to consider, cafeterias must be more efficient and better organized than it used to be. Online photographs of the food, and its written description describing the ingredients and nutritional value of the food help students make better choices when deciding what to eat. Each students will be provided with a school cafeteria account that contains any dietary restrictions. It also makes paying for food easier.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    We have implemented a system to let the parent and staff know when a child has not come to school. This can tell the parents where their children are and prevent injury. It also allows the school to know when a child has not come to school. If the child has missed school on purpose they will be given an automatic one hour detention and will be sent home with a slip that needs to be signed or else it is another one hour detention. We haven't implemented this system to be mean to the students but to ensure their wellbeing and safety.
